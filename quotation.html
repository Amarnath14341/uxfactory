Jai Exports and Imports incorporated in May 2020. Jai Exports and Imports is an initiative focusing on providing Agro & food services. With huge experience in the food industry, Jai Exports and Imports are a leading exporter of all kinds of Indian spices, pulses, grains, Fruits, Vegetables, and many more, to a number of destinations around the globe. ​With several partnerships with buyers across multiple countries, we have grown to be among the go-to companies for food export from India.
